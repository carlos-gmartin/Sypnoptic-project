The Challenge

This design challenge was presented to us by Engineers without borders and we were given a design brief which included the information about Lobitos and Piedritas, a small coastal region in Peru, and the issues they are currently facing. Our task was to understand the issues and implement our own ideas to help and improve the issues they are facing from the design brief.

Our Goal

Our main goals for this project include preventing overfishing in certain areas, gathering more knowledge for the fishermen, maintenance and increased ecotourism income from well kept environments, increased working possibilities and more. We hope to make it easier for the people living in Lobitos by providing them with more advanced knowledge of the ocean and fishing spots so that they have an easier and more efficient fishing experience.

Live updates

Cat Fish

The Cat Fish is found in the Peruvian waters. There are many different types of cat fish, of many different colours, it is a large and powerful predator usually feasting on smaller fish. A more common species found compared to the others.

Last updated 10 mins ago
Peacock Bass

Only the most experienced fishermen can catch a Peacock bass, it is known as the ultimate prize for any fishermen. This fish is a fighter and has the power to straighten hooks, destroy lures and break lines, something a beginner fishermen should avoid to prevent additional cost.

Last updated 8 mins ago
Pacu

This fish can grow up to 50 lbs in size, a very desired fish for it delicious meat, making it a fierce fighter. Many believe it could be a harder catch than the Peacock fish, something you should watch out for.
