Cat Fish

The Cat Fish is found in the Peruvian waters. There are many different types of cat fish, of many different colours, it is a large and powerful predator usually feasting on smaller fish. A more common species found compared to the others.

Last updated 10 mins ago
Peacock Bass

Only the most experienced fishermen can catch a Peacock bass, it is known as the ultimate prize for any fishermen. This fish is a fighter and has the power to straighten hooks, destroy lures and break lines, something a beginner fishermen should avoid to prevent additional cost.

Last updated 8 mins ago
Pacu

This fish can grow up to 50 lbs in size, a very desired fish for it delicious meat, making it a fierce fighter. Many believe it could be a harder catch than the Peacock fish, something you should watch out for.
